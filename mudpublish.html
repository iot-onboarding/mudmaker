Publishing your work to Github.

When you click on the publish button, you will be redirected to Github, which will request your username and password. The following actions will then take place:

  • The iot-onboarding/mudfiles repository will be cloned into your directory.
  • A new entry will be committed in mudfiles/{yourmanufacter}/{yourmodel}. This signed entry will have a new mudurl location and appropriate signature.
  • A pull request will be generated.
  • The request will be reviewed for incorporation into the main branch.

Click the "publish" button if you are ready.
