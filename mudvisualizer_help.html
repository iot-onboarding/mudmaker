Why MUD Visualizer?

MUD files are difficult to read and validate manually. This tool tries to facilitate the reading and validating process by visualizing them.

  • How to
  • MUD Visualizer Supports 6 MUD abstractions including, domain-names, manufacturer, same-manufacturer, local-networks, controller and my-controller
  • MUD Visualizer can show both the incoming and outgoing traffic which could possibly have different rules.
  • MUD Visualizer can visualize multiple MUD-files at the same time and visualize the communications based on how their ACEs match.
